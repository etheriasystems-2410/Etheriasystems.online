**Rules Content:**

1. **Eligibility**: Open to individuals aged 18 and older worldwide. Void where prohibited by law.

2. **Entry Period**: The contest begins on the announcement date and ends as specified in the promotional materials.

3. **How to Enter**: Participants must follow the official Etheria Systems social media accounts, like the contest post, and tag two friends in the comments.

4. **Winner Selection**: One winner will be selected randomly from all eligible entries using a certified random selection tool.

5. **Prize**: The winner receives a lifetime subscription to all current and future Etheria Systems products, including Quantum AI, Arcanum, DeadSpeak, Mastering the Cards, and Etheria.

6. **Notification**: The winner will be announced on our official social media channels within 7 days of the contest closing date.

7. **Claiming Prize**: The winner must claim their prize within 30 days of notification by contacting us via email.

8. **General**: Etheria Systems reserves the right to modify or cancel the contest at any time. Decisions are final.
